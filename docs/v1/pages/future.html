Future Enhancements

Roadmap

v1.0 is a solid foundation. Here's what comes next as the system matures and we learn from real data.

Phase 2: Enrichment & Feedback

Add External Data Enrichment

Integrate APIs like Clearbit or Apollo to auto-populate missing fields and validate company data.

Impact: Fewer manual lookups, richer lead profiles, better scoring accuracy.

Implement Feedback Loop

Track outcomes: which leads converted, which were recycled. Feed this back into scoring weights.

Impact: Rules improve over time based on actual conversions, not just assumptions.

CRM Integration

Push qualified leads directly to HubSpot or Salesforce instead of just Google Sheets.

Impact: Sales teams work in their native CRM. Less manual data entry.

Phase 3: AI & Machine Learning

LLM-Based Intent Classification

Use Claude or GPT to analyze lead messages for intent signals beyond keyword matching.

Impact: More nuanced scoring. Catches intent hidden in natural language (not just keywords).

Hybrid Scoring Model

Combine rule-based scoring (explainable) with ML predictions (pattern-based).

Impact: Best of both worlds. Rules + data-driven signals.

Predictive Conversion Modeling

Train a model on historical lead data to predict likelihood of close.

Impact: Rank leads by conversion probability, not just qualification score.

Known Limitations Today

No Learning

Rules are static. We manually adjust weights based on sales feedback, not data.

No Enrichment

We can't validate company size or add firmographic data from external APIs.

Keyword-Only Intent

We only look for exact keywords ("AI", "Automation"). Misses implied intent.

Google Sheets Scaling

Works for thousands of leads. Above 100k rows, need a proper database.

Form Structure Dependency

If form labels change, extraction breaks. Need a more robust field mapper.

No CRM Integration

Sales teams access Google Sheets instead of working in HubSpot or Salesforce.

When to Upgrade

  • Phase 2 (6+ months): Once you have 1000+ leads and can measure conversion rates
  • Phase 3 (12+ months): Once Phase 2 data shows clear patterns and feedback loop is working

Don't over-engineer early. Start with rules, measure outcomes, then add sophistication.
