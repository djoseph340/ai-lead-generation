Overview

Purpose

We built this system to handle lead intake at scale. Sales teams need qualified prospects, not a mountain of unfiltered form submissions.

This workflow reads inbound leads, applies a consistent scoring system, and automatically routes them to the right teams or buckets based on fit.

Why Not Use ML?

We chose rule-based scoring for three reasons:

  • Explainability. You can see exactly why a lead got 75 points instead of 50.
  • Speed. No model training, no data pipelines. Rules evaluate in milliseconds.
  • Control. Update scoring weights without hiring a data scientist.

Core Workflow

  1. Lead arrives via webhook from a form (Tally, Typeform, etc.)
  2. Fields are extracted and normalized (handles different form structures)
  3. Score is calculated based on rules (industry, company size, message content)
  4. Lead is routed to Qualified or Unqualified sheet in Google Sheets
  5. Unqualified leads get classified with tier and reason codes

Current Scope (v1.0)

This version focuses on:

  • Form ingestion via webhook
  • Rule-based lead scoring
  • Two-level classification (qualified / unqualified with tiers)
  • Persistent storage in Google Sheets
Key Metrics
Scoring Threshold 80 points
Priority Tiers 3 levels (High, Medium, Low)
Output Sheets 2 tabs (Qualified, Unqualified)
