Routing Decisions

Where Do Leads Go?

After a lead is scored, it follows one of two paths based on the score threshold.

Path 1: Qualified Leads (Score ≥ 80)

Direct to Sales

These leads are appended directly to the Leads tab in Google Sheets. No additional classification.

Fields Stored:
  • Name
  • Company
  • Email
Also Stored:
  • Industry
  • Lead Score
  • Priority (High)
  • Timestamp

Action: Sales team receives these in Google Sheets and prioritizes outreach.

Path 2: Unqualified Leads (Score < 80)

Tiered Classification

These leads go through a second classification step and are stored on the Unqualified tab with tier and reason codes.

Tier A: Close Miss

Score 70-79. Good fit but just missed the cutoff.

Tier B: Weak Fit

Score 50-69. Some signals but not enough alignment.

Tier C: No Fit

Score below 50. Minimal match with criteria.

Reason Codes (Examples):
  • insufficient_score
  • weak_role_fit
  • wrong_industry

Action: Marketing team can nurture these leads or re-score them manually.

Routing Logic (n8n IF Node)

IF (lead_score >= 80)
  → Append to Sheets "Leads" tab
ELSE
  → Classify with tier and reasons
  → Append to Sheets "Unqualified" tab

Data Flow Summary

Qualified Path

Score ≥ 80 → Leads Sheet → Sales Outreach

Unqualified Path

Score < 80 → Classify → Unqualified Sheet → Marketing Nurture
