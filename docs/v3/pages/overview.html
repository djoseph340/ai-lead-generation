System Overview - NextGenLeadGen v3.0

What Is NextGenLeadGen v3?

NextGenLeadGen v3 is an AI-powered, enterprise-grade lead qualification engine that combines intelligent enrichment, machine learning, and third-party data sources to deliver the most comprehensive lead assessment possible.

It's the evolution of v2, adding AI agents, RAG knowledge bases, and real-time enrichment APIs to handle complex qualification scenarios at scale.

Key Improvements Over v2

🤖 AI-Powered Classification

Instead of just rules, v3 uses Claude AI agents to understand intent, assess risk, and profile companies with human-like reasoning.

🧠 Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Connect your internal knowledge base—customer success stories, case studies, pricing models—to make context-aware scoring decisions.

🔗 Real-Time Enrichment APIs

Apollo for company data, Apify for web research, Clearbit for fallback. Every lead is enriched with live external data.

📊 Advanced Scoring

10+ factors (vs 6 in v2) including revenue estimation, growth trajectory, technology stack, and competitive intelligence.

🎯 Better Insights

Get reasoning for every score, AI-generated summaries, risk flags, and recommendations—not just a number.

When to Use v3 vs v2

✅ Use v3 When You Have:
  • 100+ leads per day

  • Complex qualification rules
  • Need AI-powered insights

→ Use v2 When You Have:

Architecture at a Glance

v3 follows this pipeline:

  1. Lead Arrives via webhook
  2. Initial Validation - required fields, format checks
  3. Enrichment - Apollo, Apify, Clearbit lookups (parallel)
  4. AI Classification - 3 Claude agents analyze intent, company, risk
  5. RAG Lookup - retrieve context from your knowledge base
  6. Advanced Scoring - 10+ factors with ML models
  7. Qualification - 5-tier assignment with explanation
  8. Routing - Google Sheets, HubSpot, Slack, webhooks
  9. Audit - complete decision trail for compliance
