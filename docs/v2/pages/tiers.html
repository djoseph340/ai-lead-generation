5-Tier Qualification System

The 5 Tiers

Tier 1: Highly Qualified (85+)

Action: Immediate sales outreach. Enterprise-ready leads.

Tier 2: Qualified (70-84)

Action: Sales outreach within 24h. Good fit with solid signals.

Tier 3: Warm Lead (50-69)

Action: Nurture sequence. Potential but missing key signals.

Tier 4: Cold Lead (30-49)

Action: Long-term nurture. Minimal fit.

Tier 5: Not Qualified (0-29)

Action: Archive. Does not meet criteria.
