System Overview

What Is NextGenLeadGen?

NextGenLeadGen is an automated lead qualification engine that processes form submissions and categorizes them into actionable tiers for your sales team.

It replaces manual lead review with intelligent automation, running continuously to qualify leads in real-time as they arrive.

The Problem It Solves

Challenge: Your sales team spends 40 hours/month manually reviewing 500 incoming leads.

Impact: Inconsistent quality checks, missed opportunities, wasted time on low-quality leads.

Solution: Automatic qualification. Tiers assigned in under 2 seconds per lead.

How It Works (High Level)

  1. Lead arrives via form submission (webhook)
  2. Validated for required fields and data quality
  3. Checked against duplicates and spam patterns
  4. Scored on 6 factors (industry, company size, intent, engagement, role, geography)
  5. Tier assigned based on score (T1-T5)
  6. Stored in Google Sheets with complete audit trail
  7. Ready for action by your sales team
