Workflow Architecture

Processing Pipeline

The v2 workflow consists of 10 nodes that work together to process leads from intake to qualification.

Nodes Overview

1. Webhook: Lead Intake

Receives incoming leads via HTTP POST. Configured to accept form submissions from Tally, Typeform, or custom webhooks.

2. Validate & Sanitize Input

Validates required fields, formats data, sanitizes email/phone, and checks data quality.

3. Validation Gate

If/Then node that routes valid leads forward or rejects invalid ones.

4. Duplicate & Risk Detection

Checks for duplicates in the last 100 leads and calculates risk score.

5. Advanced Multi-Factor Scoring

Scores the lead across 6 dimensions: industry, company size, intent, engagement, role, geography.

6. Intelligent Tier & Status Assignment

Assigns leads to 5 tiers and determines recommended actions based on score.

7. Compile Lead Profile

Creates comprehensive lead profile with all enrichment, scoring, and qualification data.

8. Append to Google Sheets

Writes qualified leads to the Leads tab in your Google Sheets.

9. Append to Audit Log

Logs all processing decisions to the Audit Log tab for compliance.

10. Return Success Response

Sends confirmation back to the webhook source with lead tier and score.

Data Flow

The workflow processes lead data through a series of validation and enrichment steps, ultimately producing a rich lead profile that's ready for sales.

Error Handling

Invalid leads are rejected at the validation gate with a detailed error response explaining what validation failed.
