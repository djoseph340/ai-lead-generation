What Is NextGenLeadGen?

An enterprise-grade workflow that automatically qualifies leads in real-time. It validates input, detects duplicates, scores leads across 6 dimensions, assigns them to tiers, and stores everything with full audit trail.

Built for businesses that receive hundreds of leads monthly and need to qualify them without manual review.

Key Features

Multi-Factor Scoring

6 dimensions (industry, company size, intent, engagement, role, geography) with weighted calculation.

5-Tier Classification

Granular lead prioritization from Tier 1 (Highly Qualified) to Tier 5 (Not Qualified).

Comprehensive Validation

Email regex, phone sanitization, employee count parsing, required field checking.

Fraud Prevention

Email deduplication, domain frequency analysis, spam pattern detection, risk scoring.

CRM Integration Ready

Structured output for Salesforce, HubSpot, and custom CRMs. Lead IDs for tracking.

GDPR/CCPA Compliant

Full audit trail, IP logging, processing timestamps, decision history. Compliance-ready.
